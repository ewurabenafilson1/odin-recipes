Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rotini pasta (or any short pasta)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, cubed (or mini mozzarella balls)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley or basil, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar (or lemon juice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Cook the pasta.

    Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and rinse under cold water to cool it down quickly.

  2. Prepare the vegetables.

    While the pasta cooks, wash and chop the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and olives. Cut the cheese into small cubes if needed.

  3. In a small bowl or jar, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar (or lemon juice), mustard (if using), salt, and pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste.

  4. Combine all ingredients.

    In a large mixing bowl, add the cooled pasta, chopped vegetables, cheese, and fresh herbs. Pour the dressing over the salad.

  5. Toss the salad.

    Gently toss everything together until the pasta is well coated and the ingredients are evenly distributed.

  6. Chill before serving.

    Cover the bowl and refrigerate the salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.

  7. Serve and enjoy.

    Give it a final toss before serving. Optionally garnish with extra herbs or a sprinkle of grated cheese.
